The Best Movies According to Tiffany

My top 3 movies of all-time.

La La Land

Saw this when I was 14 and it got me into movies so it holds a special place in my heart.

Anatomy of a Fall

Courtroom drama that questions the nature of truth and the different sides to people and their relationships. By turns thrilling and moving.

Nomadland

Just beautiful, I always cry loads.
